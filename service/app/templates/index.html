LTH Course Stats

Please upload your student result pdf generated with course codes.

How to generate the PDF

  1. Log on to www.student.lu.se
  2. Navigate to INFORMATION ABOUT STUDIES -> Certificates and transcripts
  3. Press the Result certificate dropdown-button
  4. Tick exactly one of box: the View course codes box
  5. Press English or Swedish to download the PDF in the correct format

The generated PDF should now be in the correct format.
